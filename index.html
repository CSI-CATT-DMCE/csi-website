CSI-CATT-DMCE


About Us

Year of Establishment: 2012

Faculty Coordinator: Dr. Chhaya Pawar

CSI DMCE - Student branch, established in the year 2012, conducts various workshops, seminars, technical competitions and also Technical fest in association with Computer Association for Technological Trends (CATT). The committee continuously works for fostering experiences, teamwork, networking etc, in our college.

It is the committee responsible for organising all the department level events like workshops, seminars, competitions, Teacher’s day celebrations. CSI CATT also contributes largely in organising events of Technitude(Technical Festival of DMCE). All efforts are very well encouraged by "Dr. Chhaya Pawar (Faculty Coordinator)" and "Dr. Amol Pande (Head of Department)".

Teams

If everyone is moving forward together, then success takes care of itself.


Planning Team

Planning the standardized flow of the events. Defining roles, responsibilities & functional requirements.

Requirements

1) Active Participation in events.
2) Planning team member should voluntarily participate in planning of the events
3) An aptitude for planning events.
4) Possess excellent Planning skills, “Can do” attitude, proactive engagement, solutions seeking & a team player.


Tech Team

Tech Team is responsible for the technical aspects of all the events conducted by the committee.

Requirements

1) Comfortable with their domain
2) Solid understanding of computer science fundamentals.
3) An aptitude for learning new technologies.
4) Possess excellent communication skills, “Can do” attitude, proactive engagement, solutions seeking & a team player.


Graphics Design Team

Graphic Design Team designs posters, social media posts, brochures and much more for CSI events that run throughout the year.

Requirements

1) Collaborating and designing required graphics with a creative touch, all while maintaining the functionality.
2)Anyone with an eye for good design! (Knowledge of any graphic editing software would be preferred)
3)Possess “Can do” attitude, proactive engagement,designer & a team player.


Tech-Creativity Team

Tech Creative team edits any kind of VIDEO that is assigned to them for the CSI events that run throughout the year.Now these videos can be for Instagram Posts, Reels, Event announcements, Event Promotions and what not.

Requirements

1) Basic knowledge of Video Editing
2) Efficient with ANY of these softwares : Kinemaster Davinci Resolve
3) creating videos from the given clips OR from scratch based on the requirement of the expected output. The editing process will be given sufficient time and resources.

Sponsorship Team

Sponsorship Team is responsible for finding sponsors for the events held by CSI that will run throughout the year.

Requirements

1) You guys will be responsible to bring/approach sponsors and throw a pitch for our events.
2)Good communication skills required and should be able to type letters.

Publicity Team

Publicity Team majorly focuses on publicising the information of all the events,seminar,workshop, webinar of the CSI-CATT. Information dissemination is the major work to be undertaken.

Requirements

1) Give out information about our events to increase participation.
2) Frame interesting content to attract people online.
3) Make the crowd aware of why they should take part in our events and what are the advantages of them.





Media Team

Media Team is the one who handles all the official social media accounts of the CSI CATT committee such as - Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter etc.

RequirementS

1) Brainstorming and coming up with unique ideas as to how to attract more audience to our social media handles would be the basic tasks when you enter the committee.
2)The basic prerequisite of the members who wish to join the Media team is you should be just familiar with handling the social media accounts and should be using them daily.
